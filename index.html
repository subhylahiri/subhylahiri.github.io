I am a post-doc studying theoretical neuroscience and biophysics in the Ganguli Lab, part of the Ginzton Lab, Department of Applied Physics, Stanford University. I have received funding from a Bio-X Genentech Fellowship and the Swartz Foundation.

Before that, I got my PhD in physics at the High Energy Theory Group, Harvard University. I then studied behavioural neuroscience as a post-doc in the Samuel Lab, part of the Department of Physics and Center for Brain Science, Harvard University.
